Privacy Policy - Guest Demo Sessions

Last Updated: October 7, 2025
Version: 3.3.0

TL;DR (Summary): We collect minimal data about your demo session (session ID, features viewed, AI questions). We hash your IP address and user agent (cannot reverse them). Data is kept max 30 days and automatically deleted. You can access, export, or delete your data at any time. We never sell your data to third parties.

1. What Data We Collect

When you use our demo features without creating an account, we collect the following information:

Session Data (Automatically Collected)

  • Session ID: A random UUID to maintain your demo session
  • Created/Updated Timestamps: When your session started and last activity
  • Session Expiration: 30 minutes from last activity

Usage Data (Activity-Based)

  • Features Viewed: Which demo features you explored (chatbot, AI content, labs, etc.)
  • AI Questions: Questions you ask the demo chatbot (limited to 10 per session)
  • User Profile: Role and interests you voluntarily share (no names or emails)

Technical Data (Pseudonymized)

  • IP Address Hash: HMAC-SHA256 hash of your IP (not the actual IP address)
  • User Agent Hash: Hashed browser fingerprint for returning guest recognition
  • Country Code: Your country only (not city or precise location)
üîí Pseudonymization: We use HMAC-SHA256 hashing to pseudonymize IP addresses and user agents. This means we cannot reverse the hash to get your original IP address. We use these hashes only to recognize returning guests and provide a personalized experience.

3. Your Privacy Rights

GDPR Rights (EU Users)

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data via cookie preferences
  • Right to Erasure: Delete your data at any time (cannot be undone)
  • Right to Rectification: Update your preferences and profile
  • Right to Data Portability: Export your data in JSON or CSV format
  • Right to Object: Opt out of analytics cookies
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Change cookie preferences anytime

CCPA Rights (California Users)

  • Right to Know: See what data we collect and why
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Do Not Sell My Personal Information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Demo works without accepting cookies

PIPEDA Rights (Canada Users)

  • Individual Access: Access your personal information
  • Consent: Control how your data is used
  • Challenging Compliance: Contact our DPO with concerns

6. Data Security

Encryption

  • At Rest: PostgreSQL database encryption
  • In Transit: TLS 1.3 for all API communications (HTTPS only)

Access Controls

  • Least privilege principle for database access
  • No direct database access from frontend
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse (10 requests per session per hour)

Audit Logging

  • All privacy actions logged with tamper-proof checksums
  • Session creation, consent changes, data access, deletion requests tracked
