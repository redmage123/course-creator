## Competitor Analysis

### Teachable
- **Positioning:** Creator economy (individual course sellers)
- **Strengths:** Brand recognition, creator tools, marketplace
- **Weaknesses:** No AI generation, no Docker labs, no org management at lower tiers, B2C-focused pricing
- **Our angle:** "Teachable is for creators. TechUni is for companies."

### Thinkific
- **Positioning:** Online course businesses and coaching
- **Strengths:** Clean UX, community features, app store
- **Weaknesses:** No AI content generation, no technical lab environments, limited B2B org features
- **Our angle:** "Thinkific is for selling courses. TechUni is for training your team."

### LearnDash (WordPress)
- **Positioning:** WordPress-native LMS, large orgs
- **Strengths:** WordPress ecosystem, flexibility, established
- **Weaknesses:** Requires WordPress dev work, no AI generation, complex setup, aging UX
- **Our angle:** "LearnDash needs a developer. TechUni is live in 20 minutes."

### Udemy for Business
- **Positioning:** Off-the-shelf course library for enterprise
- **Strengths:** Content breadth, brand recognition, enterprise sales
- **Weaknesses:** Generic content, no customization, expensive, no custom course building
- **Our angle:** "Udemy gives you their courses. TechUni builds yours."

### TalentLMS
- **Positioning:** SMB LMS with good value
- **Strengths:** Value pricing, solid feature set, mobile
- **Weaknesses:** No AI generation, no lab environments, dated UX
- **Our angle:** Feature parity at similar price + AI generation + labs differentiator

### Our Differentiation Summary
TechUni Course Creator wins on three dimensions no competitor matches simultaneously:
1. **AI-native content generation** (no competitor offers this at our price point)
2. **Docker-powered hands-on labs** (unique in the SMB/mid-market segment)
3. **Lean org pricing** (Pro at €49/month vs enterprise-only competitors)

## Technical SEO Checklist

- [ ] Core Web Vitals: LCP < 2.5s, FID < 100ms, CLS < 0.1
- [ ] Sitemap.xml submitted to Google Search Console
- [ ] robots.txt configured (allow all marketing pages, block /api/)
- [ ] Schema markup: Organization, Product, FAQPage on pricing
- [ ] Open Graph + Twitter Card meta on all pages
- [ ] Canonical tags on all pages
- [ ] Internal linking: Feature pages → use case pages → blog posts
- [ ] Blog posts: minimum 1,200 words, 1 internal link per 300 words
- [ ] Image alt text: descriptive, keyword-adjacent (not stuffed)
- [ ] Page speed: compress images to WebP, lazy-load below fold
