## Strategy Summary

**Goal:** Organic lead generation through thought leadership content targeting L&D Managers, HR Directors, and Training Coordinators at 50–500 person companies.

**North Star Metric:** MQLs from organic content (target: 8/month by Month 3)

**Content Philosophy (Ogilvy + Handley):** Quality over volume. Every piece must have a specific audience, a specific job to do, and a clear CTA. We never publish to fill the calendar — we publish to move someone closer to booking a demo or starting a free trial.

## Blog Post Publishing Standards

- Minimum 1,200 words (target 1,500–2,000 for pillar content)
- SEO title: 60 chars max
- Meta description: 155 chars max
- 1 internal link per 300 words
- 1 CTA block per post (demo booking or free trial)
- Featured image: 1200×630px, branded
- Author: "TechUni Editorial Team" + bio blurb
- Review before publish: CMO quality check (target > 80% quality score)
