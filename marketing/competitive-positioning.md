## Our Positioning Statement

**For** L&D Managers and HR Directors at 50–500 person organizations
**Who need** to build custom training content without the time, cost, or vendor dependency of traditional instructional design
**TechUni Course Creator is** an AI-native course building platform with Docker-powered lab environments
**That** generates complete course drafts from a brief, enables hands-on technical training, and scales to your whole organization at a price point designed for teams, not enterprises
**Unlike** Teachable, Thinkific, or Udemy for Business
**Which** were built for individual course creators or generic content libraries, not custom organizational training

## TechUni vs Teachable

### The Core Difference
Teachable is a platform for individual creators selling courses online. TechUni is a platform for organizations building and delivering internal training.

They share a category name ("course builder") but serve different jobs entirely.

| Dimension | TechUni Course Creator | Teachable |
|-----------|----------------------|-----------|
| **Primary user** | L&D Manager, HR Director | Individual course creator/coach |
| **Primary use case** | Internal employee training | Selling courses to external customers |
| **AI course generation** | ✅ Full curriculum from a brief | ❌ None |
| **Docker lab environments** | ✅ Live technical practice | ❌ None |
| **Org management** | ✅ Teams, cohorts, enrollment | Limited (student management) |
| **SSO (SAML/OIDC)** | ✅ Enterprise plan | ❌ Not available |
| **White-label** | ✅ Enterprise plan | ✅ Pro+ plans |
| **Analytics** | ✅ Learner + org-level | Basic |
| **API access** | ✅ Enterprise plan | ✅ |
| **Pricing** | Free / €49 / €199 /mo | Free / $39 / $119 /mo |
| **Per-seat fees** | ❌ None | ❌ None |
| **Built for organizations** | ✅ Yes | ❌ No |

**Our message vs Teachable:** "Teachable is built for creators who sell courses. TechUni is built for companies that need training that fits their organization."

## TechUni vs LearnDash

### The Core Difference
LearnDash is a WordPress plugin. It's powerful, flexible, and completely dependent on your WordPress infrastructure, development resources, and plugin maintenance. For organizations that want to build training without a web developer, it's a poor fit.

| Dimension | TechUni Course Creator | LearnDash |
|-----------|----------------------|----------|
| **Platform** | SaaS (hosted, no setup) | WordPress plugin |
| **Setup complexity** | 20 minutes to first course | Requires WordPress + hosting + dev time |
| **AI course generation** | ✅ | ❌ |
| **Docker lab environments** | ✅ | ❌ |
| **Maintenance burden** | ❌ (we maintain it) | ✅ (you maintain plugins) |
| **SSO / org management** | ✅ Enterprise plan | Via plugins (complex) |
| **Pricing** | €49/month | $199/year (plugin only; add hosting + dev) |
| **Mobile / modern UX** | ✅ | Depends on WordPress theme |

**Our message vs LearnDash:** "LearnDash is flexible if you have a developer. TechUni is live in 20 minutes if you don't."

## TechUni vs Udemy for Business

### The Core Difference
Udemy for Business is a library of pre-made courses. You don't build anything — you access what's there. That works for generic skills (Excel, Python 101, leadership basics) but fails completely for anything specific to your organization: your tools, your policies, your workflows, your culture.

| Dimension | TechUni Course Creator | Udemy for Business |
|-----------|----------------------|--------------------|
| **Custom course building** | ✅ AI-generated, fully custom | ❌ Pre-made library only |
| **Content relevance to your org** | ✅ Built for your specific context | ❌ Generic, off-the-shelf |
| **Hands-on technical labs** | ✅ Docker-powered | Limited (some practice projects) |
| **Pricing** | €49–199/month | ~$360/user/year (minimum 5 users) |
| **Org-specific content** | ✅ Full control | ❌ None |
| **Analytics** | ✅ Per-learner, per-course | ✅ Available |
| **Best for** | Custom internal training | Generic skills library |

**Cost comparison:** Udemy for Business at 50 users = ~$18,000/year. TechUni Pro = €588/year. With custom content included.

**Our message vs Udemy for Business:** "Udemy gives you their courses. TechUni builds yours."

## TechUni vs TalentLMS

### The Core Difference
TalentLMS is the closest competitor in the SMB LMS market — good value, solid feature set, decent UX. The gap is AI generation and hands-on labs. TalentLMS requires you to build all content manually or import SCORM packages. No AI assist. No live lab environments.

| Dimension | TechUni Course Creator | TalentLMS |
|-----------|----------------------|----------|
| **AI course generation** | ✅ Full curriculum from brief | ❌ None |
| **Docker lab environments** | ✅ Live technical labs | ❌ None |
| **Pricing (SMB)** | €49/month | $69–149/month (per-user tiers) |
| **Per-seat fees** | ❌ Flat rate | ✅ Per-user pricing |
| **SSO** | ✅ Enterprise plan | ✅ Available |
| **SCORM support** | Roadmap | ✅ Full SCORM |
| **Content library** | Build-your-own + AI | Limited built-in library |
| **Mobile app** | Web-responsive | ✅ Native app |
| **Gamification** | Roadmap | ✅ Available |

**Where we win:** AI generation is a step-function improvement in content creation speed. Labs are unmatched for technical training. Flat pricing is a significant advantage as teams grow.

**Where TalentLMS wins:** SCORM support (important for orgs with existing content libraries), native mobile app, gamification, more established product.

**Our message vs TalentLMS:** "TalentLMS is a solid LMS. TechUni is what you use when you want AI to build the courses and Docker to run the labs."
