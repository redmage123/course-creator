## Content Standards

### Every Post Must Have:
- Target keyword in H1, first 100 words, and at least 2 H2s
- Meta title + description following on-page-seo.md specs
- At least 1 internal link to a product/feature page
- At least 1 external link to authoritative source
- CTA button or inline CTA (free trial / demo)
- Featured image (1200×630px) with alt text
- Schema markup (Article or HowTo where applicable)
- Estimated read time in header
- Author bio (can be TechUni Team)

### Word Count Targets:
- Pillar content: 2,500–4,000 words
- Comparison articles: 1,800–2,500 words
- How-to guides: 1,200–2,000 words
- Thought leadership: 900–1,500 words
- Glossary/listicle: 2,000–3,500 words
