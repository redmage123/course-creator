## LinkedIn Strategy

### Target Audience Segments

**Primary: L&D Professionals**
- Job titles: L&D Manager, Learning & Development Director, Training Manager, Instructional Designer
- Company size: 50–5,000 employees
- Industries: Tech, Finance, Healthcare, Consulting, Manufacturing
- Pain points: Course creation takes too long, LMS is expensive, measuring ROI is hard

**Secondary: HR Directors & People Ops**
- Job titles: HR Director, VP People, Chief People Officer, People Operations Manager
- Pain points: Onboarding is inconsistent, compliance training is a headache, scaling training costs

**Tertiary: EdTech Decision Makers**
- Job titles: CTO/CEO at EdTech company, Product Manager (Education), Head of Education
- Pain points: Building internal tooling vs buying, need white-label, need AI integration

### LinkedIn Content Mix (per week)
- 2× Educational posts (AI/LMS/training tips, no direct product push)
- 1× Product showcase (feature demo, customer win, new release)
- 1× Thought leadership (industry opinion, data, prediction)
- 1× Engagement post (poll, question, discussion starter)

### LinkedIn Tactics
- **Employee advocacy**: Get team members to reshare key posts within first 30 min
- **Comment strategy**: CEO/founder comments on major EdTech posts to drive visibility
- **LinkedIn Newsletter**: Launch "The L&D Brief" — weekly AI in training newsletter
- **LinkedIn Events**: Monthly "AI in Training" live sessions (30 min, free)
- **Targeted connections**: Connect with 20 L&D professionals per week, genuine outreach

### LinkedIn Profile Optimization
- Company page banner: "Build Courses with AI. Ship in Minutes."
- Tagline: "AI-powered LMS | Docker Labs | Corporate Training | Free Plan"
- About section: Lead with problem → solution → social proof → CTA
- Featured section: Product demo video, pricing page, top blog post
- Products tab: Add TechUni Free, Pro, Enterprise as LinkedIn Products

## X/Twitter Strategy

### Target Audience Segments

**Primary: EdTech Community**
- Accounts like @edtechhub, @ISTE, @EdSurge, educators with large followings
- Conversation topics: AI in education, LMS frustrations, course design

**Secondary: AI in Education Discourse**
- AI researchers, AI builders, LLM hobbyists who care about practical AI applications
- Conversation: GPT for education, prompt engineering for courses, AI tutors

**Tertiary: Developers / Technical Trainers**
- DevRel, bootcamp instructors, Docker/Linux enthusiasts, programming educators
- Conversation: Docker, Kubernetes, hands-on coding education

### X/Twitter Content Mix (per day)
- Morning (9am): Educational tweet or hot take (standalone)
- Afternoon (2pm): Product/community/reply-driven post
- Threads: 1–2 per week on meaty topics

### X/Twitter Tactics
- **Reply to conversations**: Daily reply to 5 relevant conversations in EdTech/AI
- **Quote-tweet with commentary**: Take EdTech news and add TechUni angle
- **Build in public**: Share building stories, metrics, lessons
- **AI education demos**: Short screen recordings of AI course generation
- **Engage influencers**: @mention relevant EdTech voices in useful threads

### Account Setup
- Handle: @TechUniAI (or @TechUni_AI)
- Bio: "AI-powered course creation + Docker labs for technical training 🎓 Free plan → cc.techuni.ai"
- Pinned tweet: Product demo thread or "why we built TechUni" thread
- Banner: Clean product screenshot with tagline

## Bluesky Strategy

### Target Audience Segments

**Primary: Tech-Forward Educators**
- Software developers who teach, bootcamp instructors, technical writers
- Values: Open standards, anti-corporate-platform, hands-on learning

**Secondary: Open-Web EdTech Advocates**
- People frustrated with mainstream LMS platforms (Teachable, Thinkific, etc.)
- Interested in self-hosted, privacy-respecting, flexible tools

**Tertiary: AI/ML Community Adjacent**
- People building or using AI tools, interested in practical applications

### Bluesky Content Mix (per day)
- 1× post — mix of: technical insights, honest founder stories, community questions, product dev updates

### Bluesky Tactics
- **Join starter packs**: Create and join EdTech + AI education starter packs
- **Feeds**: Subscribe to and post to #EdTech, #AIinEducation, #eLearning feeds
- **Authenticity first**: No marketing speak — talk like a person building something
- **Engage natively**: Reply to educators sharing frustrations with existing tools
- **Cross-post selectively**: Only the most relevant X posts; Bluesky audience dislikes auto-cross-posting

### Account Setup
- Handle: @techuni.ai (custom domain handle)
- Bio: "AI courses + Docker labs for serious technical training. Building in public. Free plan at cc.techuni.ai"
- No banner needed initially — keep it personal/minimal

## Cross-Platform Coordination

### Content Repurposing Flow
```
Blog post → LinkedIn long-form post → Twitter thread → Bluesky summary
```
