## 1. Executive Summary

### The Opportunity

Course Creator is an AI-powered corporate training platform that solves three critical problems:

1. **Content Creation Bottleneck**: Traditional course creation takes 40-200 hours; we reduce this to under 1 hour
2. **Skills Gap Crisis**: 44% of workplace skills will change by 2028, requiring faster training adaptation
3. **Technical Training Gap**: No existing platform offers integrated hands-on lab environments

### Our Solution

- **AI Course Generation**: Generate complete courses from a single prompt
- **Integrated Lab Environments**: Real VSCode, JupyterLab, IntelliJ containers for hands-on learning
- **RAG-Powered AI Assistant**: Personalized learning support using knowledge graphs
- **All-in-One Platform**: Replaces 3-5 separate tools (LMS + authoring + labs + analytics)

| Annual Recurring Revenue | $49M |
| Total Customers | 1,800+ |

3. **Remote/Hybrid Work**
   - Digital training essential for distributed teams

   - GDPR, CCPA, industry regulations driving mandatory training

5. **Developer Shortage**
   - 1.4M unfilled software jobs by 2028 (US alone)

   - No good hands-on platforms exist
